Gatien Orti-vinas

I was born and raised in the South of France, where I learned to cook when I was six years old with my grandmother. Passionate about the flavor and the smell of Mediterranean cuisine, at fourteen years old, I decided to be a chef. After finishing school and traveling around the Mediterranean, I decided to go to Southeast Asia, where I became a chef in a luxury resort in Thailand.

I have spent time in luxury resorts, hotels, and freestanding restaurants. Every step I have taken has been an experience to create my own cuisine.
